Belgian Air Force Operations & Aircraft

Aircraft Fleet

The Belgian Air Force operates a diverse range of modern aircraft designed for air defense, transport, training, and support missions.

F-16 Fighting Falcon

Primary fighter aircraft for air defense and tactical operations. Proven combat capability and advanced avionics systems.

Aérospatiale AS532

Transport and utility helicopter providing airlift capability and support for military operations across the nation.

Airbus A400M

Strategic airlift transport aircraft for cargo and personnel movement supporting national operations and cooperative missions.

Operations & Training

Professional military training and operational readiness maintained through continuous development and mission preparation.

Pilot Training

Comprehensive flight training programs developing the next generation of Belgian Air Force pilots and aviation professionals.

Combat Operations

Advanced tactical training and combat readiness exercises ensuring peak operational effectiveness and mission capability.

Joint Exercises

International joint exercises and training operations enhancing interoperability with allied air forces across Europe.
