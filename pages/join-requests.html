Join Requests

Guidance for joining the Belgian Air Force virtual MILSIM.

Request to Join

Submit a join request to be considered for membership, training, or operational assignment. The Belgian Air Force team reviews applications, evaluates qualifications, and helps guide new members through onboarding.

What to Include

Provide your name, experience, preferred role, and any relevant background. Clear requests help the recruitment team place you in the right unit and training pipeline.
