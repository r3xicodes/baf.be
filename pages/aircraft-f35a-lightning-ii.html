F-35A Lightning II

Factsheet for Belgium's advanced stealth multirole fighter.

Role

The F-35A provides advanced stealth air dominance, precision strike capability, and advanced sensor fusion for Belgian Air Force operations.

Crew

Single-seat cockpit designed for advanced avionics and networked mission systems, flown by highly trained stealth fighter pilots.

Speed / Range

High subsonic cruise with supersonic dash capability; effective mission radius of roughly 600 nautical miles in typical operations.

Call Sign Usage

Typically uses squadron-based call signs such as "Lightning 1" for lead and "Lightning 2" for wingman, reflecting the stealth fighter fleet's naming convention.

Standard Missions

  • Stealth penetration and strike
  • Air superiority and suppression of air defenses
  • Networked ISR and battle management
  • Defense of Belgian airspace

Squadron Assignment

F-35A aircraft are assigned to forward-deployed fighter squadrons capable of rapid independent response operations.

Notes for GeoFS Pilots

  • Practice sensor fusion and target handoff in multiplayer missions.
  • Use low-observable tactics when flying contested airspace scenarios.
  • Take advantage of advanced datalink support for joint strike coordination.
  • Respect maintenance and sortie planning limitations in simulated operations.
