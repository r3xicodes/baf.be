Dassault Falcon 7X

Factsheet for the Belgian Air Force executive and VIP transport jet.

Role

Used primarily for VIP transport, government missions, and strategic liaison flights within Belgian and allied networks.

Crew

Pilot, co-pilot, and cabin crew configured to support executive passenger transport and diplomatic missions.

Speed / Range

Long-range business jet performance, with a cruise speed of around Mach 0.82 and transcontinental capability for extended missions.

Call Sign Usage

Flight callsigns use mission identifiers and priority handling descriptions for government transport missions.

Standard Missions

  • Dignitary and government transport
  • Diplomatic travel and VIP support
  • Rapid liaison flights for government and command operations

Squadron Assignment

Operated by the VIP transport wing with basing at Melsbroek Air Base.

Notes for GeoFS Pilots

  • Focus on smooth, stable flight profiles and passenger comfort in simulation missions.
  • Plan alternate landing sites and cross-border clearances for diplomatic routes.
  • Observe proper ATC coordination and VIP movement procedures.
  • Use the Falcon 7X for high-priority support missions and protocol scenarios.
