Opportunities in the Belgian Air Force

The United States Air Force offers diverse career opportunities for qualified individuals. Whether you're interested in flying, engineering, medical services, or administration, the Air Force has a role for you.

Our personnel receive comprehensive training, competitive benefits, and the opportunity to serve their country while developing valuable skills and experience.

Career Paths

Pilot & Flight Officer

Lead advanced aircraft and unmanned systems. Requires college degree and flight training qualification. Competitive flight pay and benefits.

Engineering & Technical

Design, develop, and maintain advanced aerospace systems. Ideal for computer science, electrical, and mechanical engineers.

Medical Services

Provide medical care to Air Force personnel worldwide. Positions available for doctors, nurses, dental professionals, and support staff.

Intelligence & Operations

Analyze intelligence data and conduct operations planning. Key role in national security and military strategy.

Logistics & Supply

Manage supply chains and ensure readiness. Critical role in supporting global Air Force operations.

Administration & Support

Provide administrative, financial, and personnel support. Essential services that keep the Air Force operating efficiently.

Benefits & Compensation

Competitive Salary: Competitive military pay based on rank and experience, with regular pay increases and promotions.

Healthcare: Comprehensive health insurance coverage for you and your family, including medical, dental, and vision.

Retirement Benefits: Defined benefit pension plan with 20 years of service eligibility.

Education & Training: Tuition assistance, GI Bill benefits, and continuous professional development opportunities.

Housing & Allowances: Basic Allowance for Housing (BAH) when not provided, plus Family Separation Allowance when applicable.

Leave & Time Off: 30 days of paid leave annually, plus federal holidays and special duty leave.

Basic Requirements

  • U.S. Citizenship or permanent resident status
  • Valid passport and willing to serve worldwide
  • Pass security clearance investigation
  • Meet height, weight, and physical fitness standards
  • Pass medical examination
  • High School diploma or GED equivalent
  • Willing to serve Active Duty or Reserve component

Contact a local recruiter for more information about specific positions and requirements.
