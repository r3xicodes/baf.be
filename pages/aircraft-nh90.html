NHIndustries NH90

Factsheet for Belgium's NH90 tactical helicopter.

Role

The NH90 provides tactical transport, search and rescue, and maritime support for Belgian Air Force operations.

Crew

Typically includes two pilots and one or two mission specialists depending on mission type.

Speed / Range

Modern medium-lift helicopter with cruise speed suited for tactical insertion and airlift operations over 500 km.

Call Sign Usage

Uses tactical rotorcraft call signs and may switch between maritime and land-based identifiers for flexibility.

Standard Missions

  • Tactical transport and troop insertion
  • Search and rescue
  • Medical evacuation
  • Maritime patrol support

Squadron Assignment

NH90 helicopters operate from Beauvechain and Koksijde bases within the Air Mobility Wing.

Notes for GeoFS Pilots

  • Practice low-altitude insertion techniques over terrain and sea.
  • Manage fuel carefully on longer patrol routes.
  • Use navigation aids for mountain and coastal operations.
  • Coordinate closely with ground and naval units during joint missions.
