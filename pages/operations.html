BAF Operations Overview

The Belgian Air Force operates multiple tactical wings and support commands across Belgian territory and allied locations. Our operations focus on air superiority, multirole strike operations, air defense, close air support, strategic and tactical airlift, helicopter operations, pilot training, and reconnaissance capabilities.

We maintain continuous operational readiness and are committed to defending Belgian airspace, maintaining sovereign operations, and contributing to international security operations. The force operates under independent command structures with allied nations across Europe.

Tactical Wings and Squadrons

1st Tactical Wing - Florennes

1st Fighter Squadron: Air Superiority operations with F-16 Fighting Falcon aircraft. 2nd Fighter Squadron: Multirole Strike operations with F-16 Fighting Falcon aircraft.

2nd Tactical Wing - Kleine-Brogel

3rd Fighter Squadron: Quick Reaction Alert (QRA) and Air Defense with F-16 Fighting Falcon aircraft. 4th Fighter Squadron: Close Air Support operations with F-16 Fighting Falcon aircraft.

15th Air Transport Wing - Melsbroek

20th Transport Squadron: Strategic Airlift operations with Airbus A400M Atlas aircraft. 21st Transport Squadron: Tactical Airlift operations with C-130 Hercules aircraft.

Air Mobility Wing - Beauvechain

40th Helicopter Squadron: Search & Rescue and Utility operations with NH90 Helicopter. 41st Helicopter Squadron: Tactical Transport operations with NH90 Helicopter.

Training Command

50th Training Squadron: Pilot Training programs with SF-260 trainer aircraft. 51st Advanced Squadron: Advanced Jet Training with Alpha Jet aircraft.

Air Support Command

60th UAV Squadron: Intelligence, Surveillance & Reconnaissance with MQ-9 SkyGuardian aircraft. 61st Support Squadron: Logistics and Ground Support operations.

Operational Roles

Air Superiority: Maintaining dominance in air operations to ensure freedom of operations for Belgian forces through F-16 and future F-35 fighter squadrons.

Multirole Strike: Providing tactical strike capabilities supporting ground operations and international missions across multiple theaters.

Air Defense: Quick Reaction Alert (QRA) and continuous air defense operations protecting Belgian airspace and national territory.

Strategic Airlift: Heavy transport capabilities with the Airbus A400M Atlas for strategic deployment of personnel and equipment.

Helicopter Operations: Search & Rescue, utility transport, and tactical helicopter operations supporting military and civilian requirements.

Intelligence & Reconnaissance: Advanced ISR capabilities through MQ-9 Reaper UAV operations for reconnaissance and surveillance missions.
