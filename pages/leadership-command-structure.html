Command Structure

Full chain of command for the Belgian Air Force GeoFS site.

Command Overview

The Belgian Air Force operates under a structured command organization that emphasizes readiness, interoperability, and mission control.

Air Force Headquarters

Responsible for strategic direction, planning, and overall force management.

Operational Commands

Manage tactical wings, mission scheduling, and international mission contributions.

Training Commands

Oversee pilot training, instructor development, and qualification standards.

Support Commands

Provide logistics, air traffic control, maintenance, and base support services.

Key Leadership Roles

Chief of Air Force – Overall military leader of the Belgian Air Force.

Deputy Command – Second-in-command and operational readiness lead.

Senior Leadership Team – Includes directors for operations, logistics, training, ISR, and support services.
