Sexual Misconduct & Disciplinary Actions

Policy and reporting procedures for maintaining a professional and respectful environment.

Zero Tolerance Policy

The Belgian Air Force maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy toward sexual harassment and misconduct. All personnel are expected to maintain professional standards and treat colleagues with dignity and respect.

Reporting Procedures

File a Report

Submit reports to your immediate supervisor, unit commander, or designated Equal Opportunity Officer through official channels.

Confidential Support

Confidential support and counseling services are available to all personnel through the BAF Support Services office.

Investigation Process

All reports are investigated thoroughly by trained personnel. Retaliation is strictly prohibited and subject to disciplinary action.

Disciplinary Framework

Substantiated violations of conduct standards result in disciplinary action ranging from counseling to termination of service, depending on severity and circumstances.

  • Level 1: Counseling and education for first-time minor violations
  • Level 2: Written reprimand and mandatory training for moderate violations
  • Level 3: Suspension and formal disciplinary action for serious violations
  • Level 4: Discharge from service for severe or repeated violations

Support Resources

Personnel affected by sexual misconduct or harassment have access to:

  • 24/7 Victim Support Services
  • Mental Health and Counseling Services
  • Legal Assistance
  • Medical Care and Advocacy
