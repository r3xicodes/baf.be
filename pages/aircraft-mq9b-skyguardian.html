MQ-9B SkyGuardian

Factsheet for the Belgian Air Force long-endurance ISR unmanned aircraft.

Role

The MQ-9B performs intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance, and target acquisition missions in support of national and independent air operations.

Crew

Uncrewed aircraft controlled remotely by sensor operators and mission commanders on the ground.

Speed / Range

Long endurance with mission persistence measured in tens of hours, optimized for persistent surveillance.

Call Sign Usage

Standard UAV call signs such as "SkyGuardian 01" are used in coordination with ATC and ISR command channels.

Standard Missions

  • Persistent ISR coverage
  • Battlefield reconnaissance
  • Target acquisition and monitoring
  • Maritime surveillance

Squadron Assignment

Operated by the UAV squadron, providing advanced ISR capabilities and based at Florennes AB.

Notes for GeoFS Pilots

  • Focus on mission planning and sensor collection rather than aircraft handling.
  • Coordinate airspace with manned fighters for safe integration.
  • Use long-duration missions to gather intelligence for allied operations.
  • Monitor fuel and sensor cycles during persistent sorties.
