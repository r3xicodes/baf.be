About the Belgian Air Force

The Belgian Air Force (Force Aérienne Belge) is the aerial warfare branch of Belgium, responsible for safeguarding national airspace, supporting ground and naval operations, and contributing to international security missions. Formally reestablished on 11 February 2025 and commanded by Lieutenant General Madeleine van der Meer since 7 April 2026.

Our core mission—defending Belgian sovereignty, maintaining air superiority, and ensuring homeland security—guides every member of the force. Based at Quartier Reine Elisabeth in Evere, Brussels, we operate fighter squadrons, transport units, helicopter squadrons, and advanced training capabilities across multiple operational bases.
